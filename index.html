Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking with us.

Customized cakes should be ordered at least two weeks in advance to help us plan well — earlier is best for weddings and large events.

Yes, we deliver everywhere within and outside the country depending on the nature of the order. Delivery is provided at the cost of the client.

Every cake is fully customizable. Share your theme, colours, flavour preferences and inspiration images, and our team will design a piece around your vision.

Payment is done strictly through mobile money.

All orders are strictly confirmed with at least part payment before complete delivery is done. Last-minute cancellations are not accepted unless made at least 3 days before the order date.

Our signature flavours include vanilla bean, rich chocolate, red velvet, lemon zest and marble, with seasonal and custom flavour options available on request.

Prices range from UGX 100,000 and above, depending on flavour, size and design details, and are subject to change in line with market price fluctuations.
